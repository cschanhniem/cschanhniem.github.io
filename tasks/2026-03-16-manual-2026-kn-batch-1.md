# 2026-03-16 Manual 2026 KN Batch 1

## Scope

Author the first `Nhập Lưu 2026` batch for `Tiểu Bộ`, starting with the full `Khuddakapāṭha` (`kp1-kp9`) in modern Vietnamese that remains recitable, doctrinally faithful, and structurally complete.

## Why This Batch

`KN` is too broad to start as a random grab bag. `Khuddakapāṭha` is the cleanest editorial foothold because it contains short root passages and widely used liturgical texts:

- `kp1` Three Refuges
- `kp2` Ten Precepts
- `kp3` Thirty-Two Parts of the Body
- `kp4` The Boy’s Questions
- `kp5` Blessings
- `kp6` Gems
- `kp7` Outside the Walls
- `kp8` A Treasure Trove
- `kp9` The Discourse on Love

This gives `KN` an initial manual layer that covers refuge, ethics, body contemplation, doctrinal summary, blessing, triple gem, dedication to the departed, merit as true wealth, and loving-kindness.

## Editorial Notes

- Short formula texts such as `kp1-kp4` were translated with a plain, ceremonial cadence rather than essay expansion.
- Verse texts such as `kp5-kp9` keep the main body of the chant intact instead of collapsing into summary prose.
- Refrains and doctrinal pivots such as the triple jewel truth formula, the highest blessing refrain, and the universal scope of mettā were preserved explicitly.
- The modernized Vietnamese aims to read smoothly aloud without slipping into vague devotional language.
