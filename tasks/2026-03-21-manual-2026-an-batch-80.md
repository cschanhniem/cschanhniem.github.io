## Editorial Notes

- `AN 3.75` keeps the pastoral force of encouragement. The discourse is not abstract faith doctrine but concrete advice about what one should establish in loved ones.
- `AN 3.76-77` are paired discourses on future existence. The first preserves the field-seed-moisture triad with consciousness as the seed. The second keeps the shift to intention and launching-point explicit instead of smoothing the two discourses into one.
- `AN 3.78` remains dialogical and discriminating. The point is not that austerities are useless, but that they are judged by whether wholesome states grow and unwholesome states fade.
- `AN 3.79` retains the fragrance simile and closing verse. The lay follower's virtue had to read expansive without turning sentimental.
- `AN 3.80` keeps cosmological scale and dramatic restraint together. The Buddha's correction to Udāyī was preserved so the discourse does not become mere marvel narrative.
- `AN 3.81-85` are the tam học cluster. Each route was kept distinct: simple injunction, donkey simile, farmer simile, Vajji monk case, and the defining marks of a trainee.
