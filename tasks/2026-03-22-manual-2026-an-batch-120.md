# Manual 2026 Translation: AN Batch 120

## Scope
- **Collection**: Aṅguttara Nikāya (AN)
- **Range**: AN 4.121 - AN 4.130
- **Vagga**: Bhayavagga (Chapter on Fears / Chương Sợ Hãi)
- **Status**: Completed

## Suttas Translated
1. **an4.121**: Kinh Tự Trách (Sợ Hãi 3) - Four fears (guilt, shame, punishment, bad rebirth).
2. **an4.122**: Kinh Sợ Hãi Sóng Cuộn - Four dangers of entering water mapped to monastic life.
3. **an4.123**: Kinh Khác Biệt (Thứ Nhất) - Four individuals, four jhānas, rebirth outcomes compared.
4. **an4.124**: Kinh Khác Biệt (Thứ Hai) - Four jhānas combined with contemplation of aggregates, leading to Pure Abodes.
5. **an4.125**: Kinh Tứ Vô Lượng Tâm (Thứ Nhất) - Four brahmavihāras, rebirth outcomes in Brahma realms compared between ordinary people and noble disciples.
6. **an4.126**: Kinh Tứ Vô Lượng Tâm (Thứ Hai) - Four brahmavihāras combined with contemplation of aggregates, leading to Pure Abodes.
7. **an4.127**: Kinh Kỳ Diệu Về Như Lai (Thứ Nhất) - Four amazing things about the Realized One's appearance (light in the darkness).
8. **an4.128**: Kinh Kỳ Diệu Về Như Lai (Thứ Hai) - Four amazing things about how people respond to the Buddha's teachings.
9. **an4.129**: Kinh Kỳ Diệu Về Tôn Giả Ānanda - Four amazing things about Ānanda (uplifting presence and speech).
10. **an4.130**: Kinh Chuyển Luân Thánh Vương (Sợ Hãi 2) - Four amazing things about a Wheel-Turning Monarch, compared to Ānanda. Includes the uddāna verse.

## Editorial Notes

- Preserved the uddāna verse at the end of AN 4.130.
