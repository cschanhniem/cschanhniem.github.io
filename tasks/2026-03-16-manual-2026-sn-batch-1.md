# 2026-03-16 Manual 2026 SN Batch 1

## Scope

Author the first substantial `Nhập Lưu 2026` batch for `Tương Ưng Bộ` in curated modern Vietnamese, keeping the doctrinal skeleton of each sutta intact while making the prose readable aloud.

## Why These Suttas

This batch is not consecutive numbering for its own sake. It establishes a doctrinal spine for `SN`:

- `sn12.2` for the analytic structure of dependent origination
- `sn12.15` for right view and the middle way beyond `có` and `không`
- `sn22.59` for the not-self characteristic across the five aggregates
- `sn35.28` for the fire discourse across the six sense bases
- `sn47.42` for satipaṭṭhāna seen through arising and ceasing conditions
- `sn56.1` for samādhi as support for seeing the four noble truths
