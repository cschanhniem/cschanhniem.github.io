## Editorial Intent

- Keep `SN` authoring on a doctrinal spine:
  - dependent origination
  - aggregates and not-self pressure points
  - the all and the senses
  - the path, the awakening factors, the faculties
  - stream-entry factors
  - the noble truths
- Favor concise, speakable Vietnamese that still preserves the doctrinal hinge of each discourse.
- For very short suttas, keep the center of gravity on the exact pivot of the original instead of inflating them with generic commentary.
