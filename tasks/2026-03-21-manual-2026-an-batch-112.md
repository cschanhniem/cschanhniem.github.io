## Editorial Notes

- `an4.41-42` were kept as concise but full doctrinal instructions:
  - four developments of immersion
  - four modes of answering questions
- `an4.43-44` are a deliberate pair. The first is framed as four kinds of people, the second as four non-wonderful and four wonderful qualities. They overlap, but should not collapse into one shell.
- `an4.45-46` are another deliberate pair. `an4.46` retells the Rohitassa exchange to the Saṅgha, so the prose should preserve the reporting frame rather than merely duplicating `an4.45`.
- `an4.47-50` move from contrast, to speaking, to cognitive distortion, to corruption of renunciants. The last two need a firmer doctrinal tone than the first two.
